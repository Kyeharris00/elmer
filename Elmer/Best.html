Welcome to Elmer's Helpers!


Elmer's Helper's was founded in January of 1989, bringing quality service to people of all colors and creeds. Founded on the bayside of Newark, New Jersey we have raised over a billion dollars to fund our organization, and every penny has been used toward helping all home owners who just need a little assistance, becuase here at Elmer's we serve you.


Who Can Apply?


We are a non for profit organization that has served worldwide for over 30 years providing people with the basic necesities for home construction and remodeling.


All Our Applicants Must adhere to the following rules:

  • • Be 21 and older

  • • Own a home

  • • Have good credit

  • • Have a form of Identification

  • • Place an Elmer's sign on their property as a form of advertisement

  • • Allow Elmer officials to take pictures of the home before and after job completion
